Fertilizer & Irrigation Guide

Fertilizer Schedule

Organic Fertilizers

  • Compost: 5 tons/acre at planting
  • Vermicompost: 2 tons/acre after 30 days

Inorganic Fertilizers

Irrigation Guide

Irrigation Schedule

  • Maturity stage: Reduce frequency
