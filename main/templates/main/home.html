Fertilizer & Irrigation Guide

Fertilizer Schedule

Organic Fertilizers

  • Compost: 5 tons/acre at planting
  • Vermicompost: 2 tons/acre after 30 days
  • Neem cake: 200 kg/acre as pest deterrent

Inorganic Fertilizers

  • NPK (10:26:26): 50 kg/acre as basal dose
  • Urea: 25 kg/acre at 30 days (top dressing)
  • DAP: 25 kg/acre at flowering stage

Irrigation Guide

Irrigation Schedule

  • Initial: Immediately after sowing
  • Vegetative stage: Every 7-10 days
  • Flowering stage: Maintain moist soil
  • Maturity stage: Reduce frequency

Water Saving Tips

  • Use drip irrigation for water efficiency
  • Apply mulch to reduce evaporation
  • Monitor soil moisture regularly
